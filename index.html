Papworth Method

Possibly helpful for asthmatics: Inhale slowly through the nose (2 seconds) and take twice as long to exhale through pursed lips (4 seconds). No hold is required for this technique, so set the timer to zero. Further reading


Box Breathing

Helpful for anxiety: Breathe in through your nose counting to 4 slowly. Hold your breath for 4 seconds. Slowly exhale through your mouth for 4 seconds. Repeat until you feel calm and re-centered. Further reading


4-7-8 Breath Method

Great for avoiding panic attacks: Breathe in through your nose to the count of 4. Hold the breath to the count of 7. Exhale through your mouth to the count of 8. You may want to try slightly different timings, 5-4-8 is a popular alternative. Further reading
